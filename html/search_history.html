Search History

Whenever you use Search, we store your search terms, your approximate location (usually between 200 and 390 square meters), and the current time (within an hour). After 7 days, we store a rougher estimate of your location (usually between 3,200 and 6,240 square meters) for 23 days. We use this information to improve your search results and our other services. If you’d like to clear this information, you can do it from Settings. Learn more
